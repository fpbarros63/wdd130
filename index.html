"Pernambuco - Recife"

"Recife is the capital of the state of Pernambuco, located in the Northeast region of Brazil. The city was founded on March 12, 1537. From the 16th century onwards, it was under Dutch rule for a period of time, during which time there was an intense process of occupation and urbanization. Recife has a humid tropical climate and a relief that varies from flat to gently undulating. It is located in the Atlantic Forest biome. Because of the rivers that cross the city and its bridges, it has been nicknamed the Brazilian Venice. This is where I live and I love this city." - https://brasilescola.uol.com.br/brasil/recife.htm
